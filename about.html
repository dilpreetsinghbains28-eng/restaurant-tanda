The Culinary Soul of Urmar Tanda

A legacy of flavors, from aromatic North Indian spices to the sizzle of Chinese woks, served with the warmth of traditional Punjabi hospitality.

Our Legacy

For over two decades, Galaxy Restaurant has stood as a cornerstone of the Urmar Tanda community. Located near the Police Station in the heart of the local market, we have grown from a modest eatery into the city's favorite destination for family gatherings and snacks.

Our journey has always been defined by a commitment to hygiene, quality ingredients, and the authentic taste of North Indian and Chinese cuisines. Every dish that leaves our kitchen is a testament to our dedication to professional culinary standards.
